Make sure your arcade is close by.

Big amusment centers may have arcades built into them, but try to go for only local small arcades. The more local it is, the better experiance you will have.

Look at ratings, not all arcades are created equal. Some are not as good as others. For their selections of games, to the food. Make sure you find what you and your friends/family would like.
